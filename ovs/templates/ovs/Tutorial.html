Tutorial


A user can register as either Admin or User/Voter.
An Admin is responsible for creating polls, adding voters and generstion results of the poll.
A Voter can register, login and vote.


Visual Cryptography

In this step the voter has to upload a pixelated image shared to the registered email ID.


After every voter has casted their vote, the administrator will be able to generate the results for the poll.
The results can be viewed by users.
